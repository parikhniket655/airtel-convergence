Addressing Household Concerns

Consolidating is a big step. Here's how we address standard consumer barriers to help you switch smoothly.

Will consolidating services really save me money?

Yes, absolutely. By combining services, we avoid redundant platform overheads. For example, buying Broadband (₹599), two Postpaid SIMs (₹700), Airtel Xstream Box (₹250), Google One 2TB storage (₹209), Xsafe Camera (₹150), and Payments Bank (₹99) individually costs ₹2,006/month. With our **Family Secure** package, you get all these for ₹1,499/month. Even better, you pay just ₹999/month for the first 3 months! Enable **Autopay** to reduce this further by ₹50/month, bringing it to ₹949/mo.

What are Thanks Coins and Google One benefits?

**Airtel Thanks Coins** is our loyalty reward program. You earn coins monthly based on your plan level, and bonus coins for activating autopay. Coins can be redeemed directly in the Airtel app for OTT upgrades, shopping coupons, and bank cashbacks.

**Google One Cloud Storage Benefits** are included in all of our packages (100GB in Starter/Connect, 200GB in Family Secure, and 2TB in Infinite). You receive active code credits allowing you to secure your photos, files, and Gmail backups.

Why should I enable Autopay, and is it secure?

Autopay ensures you never miss a payment deadline. Setting it up gives you an **instant ₹50 discount** on every single monthly invoice, plus a **1,000 Thanks Coin bonus** on activation. It is secured by **Airtel Payments Bank Safe Pay** protocols, verifying card/account credentials through encrypted networks and giving you a 4-hour pre-draft notification window so you can pause it at any time.

I dread the migration process. How complicated is the setup?

We completely eliminate migration friction. When you book a package, we assign a dedicated Airtel Concierge Engineer to your home. They will visit at your convenient time, install the high-speed fiber router, set up the Xstream Box, configure your Airtel Xsafe cameras, set up your Google One storage account, and transition your mobile numbers without a single minute of downtime.

If I put all my services under one provider, isn't that a risk?

While all your billing and support are consolidated, the services run on distinct, independent physical infrastructures:
• Your **Mobile SIMs** run on our national cellular towers.
• Your **Broadband** runs on high-grade optical fiber directly in your home.
• Your **Airtel Xstream TV** runs on hybrid satellite & fiber internet feeds.
This physical separation ensures that if your fiber is cut during road maintenance, your mobile 5G internet and satellite TV remain fully functional, and your smart camera automatically bridges connection using your mobile data pool backup.
